Controls

WASD - move forward, backward, left and right

SPACE- move up
LSHIFT - move down

Z - Increase Contrast
X - Decrease Contrast
C - Increase Colour Intensity (Brightness)
V - Decrease Colour Intensity (Brightness)
B - Increase Refraction
N - Decrease Refraction

NUMBERS 1 - 7 CHANGE THE COLOUR OF THE MONKEY HEAD
